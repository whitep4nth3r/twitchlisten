To add TwitchListen to OBS

  1. Enter your Twitch channel name, choose a theme and click listen.
  2. Grab the URL generated from your browser's address bar.
  3. And add a new browser source in OBS with that URL, choose to "Control audio via OBS" (you might also want to choose to monitor and output this audio channel).
  4. To activate the listener, right click on the browser source in OBS and choose interact.
  5. Click anywhere on the browser source to activate TwitchListen, and the message will disappear.
  6. Encourage your viewers to chat, and you'll hear TwitchListen!
